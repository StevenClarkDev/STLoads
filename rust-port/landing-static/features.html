Core Features

Built for speed, visibility, and control.

The platform is shaped around the day-to-day work of moving freight: finding capacity, dispatching cleanly, keeping records organized, and knowing what is happening at every point.

Instant Load Matching

Match freight with available carriers based on route, capacity, timing, and availability.

Real-Time Tracking

Keep shippers, carriers, and brokers aligned with live shipment progress and status updates.

Verified Carrier Network

Work with carriers that are vetted, licensed, insured, and ready for professional freight movement.

Digital Documentation

Keep BOLs, PODs, invoices, rate confirmations, and other records attached to each load.

Analytics Dashboard

Review spend, lane performance, carrier activity, and operational health from one place.

24/7 Support

Keep freight moving with support for operational issues, questions, and urgent shipment needs.
