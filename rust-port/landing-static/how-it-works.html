The Process

Simple steps. Controlled execution.

Every freight movement needs speed, accountability, and documentation. STLoads keeps each step visible so teams can act without chasing calls, spreadsheets, or scattered updates.

01

Create Your Account

Shippers, carriers, and brokers join the correct portal and keep their work separated by role.

02

Post or Find Loads

Shippers post freight, carriers browse matched opportunities, and brokers coordinate active lanes.

03

Quote and Confirm

Teams compare options, confirm terms, assign routes, and move the load into dispatch.

04

Track Every Mile

Shipment status, pickup activity, delivery updates, and important events stay visible.

05

Capture Documents

BOLs, PODs, rate confirmations, and invoices stay attached to the load record.

06

Close and Improve

Completed loads become operational history for reporting, compliance, and better decisions.
